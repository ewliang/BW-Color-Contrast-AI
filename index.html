BW Color Contrast AI

Created by Eric Liang

BW Color Contrast AI is a simple machine learning webapp that predicts what colored text best contrasts a background color. In this case, this webapp was designed for contrasting black text vs white text. You can train the algorithm by making sure the "Enable Training?" checkbox at the bottom is checked. Then it is your responsibility to select what's the best contrasting colors. Once you feel like you've trained it enough, you can uncheck the checkbox, and a border will appear around the colored box that the AI thinks is best contrast for the current color.
